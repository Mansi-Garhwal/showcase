Hello, I’m Mansi :D

I’m an architect honed by direct involvement across studios, sites, and collaborative design environments. I’ve developed a strong foundation in design, detailing, coordination, and execution, supported by the guidance and teamwork of architects at every level. These experiences have strengthened my adaptability and ability to clearly translate ideas from concept to reality.
I enjoy structured workflows, deliberate improvement, and using digital tools to communicate with precision. I believe good design should feel warm, functional, and quietly intentional. This portfolio showcases a curated selection of works that reflect my approach to thinking, learning, and ongoing growth as an architect.

Architecture, for me, is about clarity, in both process and space.
