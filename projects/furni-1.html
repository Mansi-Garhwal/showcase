Wooden Chair- Product Design

Project typeProduct Design
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atMSS Design & Lakood

This project included production drawings and 3D visualizations for a wooden chair that balances minimal design with ergonomic comfort. The chair features a curved backrest and a contoured seat, designed for comfort in both residential and professional environments. To achieve this, the detailed working drawings ensured precision in joinery, material alignment, and finish coordination. The White Oak veneer with vertical grains was specified for the seat and back, paired with a matte clear lacquered oak or Oregon pine frame to emphasize texture and natural tone in render outputs.

A technical exploration in precision and visualization, translating design intent into buildable form.
