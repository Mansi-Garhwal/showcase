Avighna Farmhouse, Chhatarpur

Project typeResidential
LocationChhatarpur, New Delhi
Involvement StageConcept Design to Construction Phase
RoleInterior Architect
During my tenure atSixteen Design Studio

Located near Lahori Gate in New Delhi, this project reinterprets the barrack typology for the Railway Protection Force through a language of strength, order, and simplicity. The design combines the sturdiness of exposed brickwork with the lightness of glass and muted tones, creating a dialogue between resilience and openness. Functionally, the layout ensures clarity, ventilation, and efficiency, integrating dormitories, shared amenities, and circulation zones that foster both privacy and camaraderie among personnel.

Grounded in sustainability and minimalism, the structure employs local materials, shaded corridors, and green courtyards to enhance comfort while reducing environmental impact. The overall composition reflects a disciplined yet humane environment that balances protection with livability.

A simple, strong, and enduring home for those who safeguard the nation’s railways.
