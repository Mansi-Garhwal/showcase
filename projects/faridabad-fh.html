Gupta Farmhouse, Faridabad

Project typeResidential
LocationFaridabad, Haryana
Involvement StageUp to Construction Phase
RoleDesign Architect
During my tenure atSixteen Design Studio

The Rammed Earth Farmhouse is conceived as a sculptural retreat where bold pitched roofs, earthy materials, and open veranda merge with the landscape. Rammed Earth walls, stone cladding, timber lined roofs, and expansive glazing anchor the home to its natural setting while opening uninterrupted views outward. Light, shadow, and poolside reflections shift across the structure throughout the day, with black metal accents adding a contemporary contrast to the warm palette.

At its center lies a courtyard wrapped in tropical greens, complete with a sunken firepit lounge that becomes the emotional core of the home. While this inward garden offers quiet refuge, the rest of the layout expands toward long vistas, water edges, and shaded leisure decks.

A crafted union of earth, timber, and landscape, where sculptural, serene, and rooted in place.
