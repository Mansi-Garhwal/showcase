Vida Villa, Aldona

Project typeResidential
LocationAldona, Goa
Involvement StageDesign Development to Construction Phase
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atMSS Design

This four-bedroom villa is a tranquil retreat overlooking expansive paddy fields, designed with a simple, site-responsive approach. Oriented East–West to capture natural breezes and views, the entry is cooled by a northeast water body, while deep balconies and whitewashed, Greek inspired forms lend a soft Mediterranean character. Interiors combine rustic warmth with modern comfort through sunlit rooms, airy verandas, and a private pool. Clear spatial flow and balanced materials create a relaxed, slow-living environment suited for both family living and an Airbnb escape.

My role involved preparing detailed working drawings and construction documentation, along with site visits for supervision and coordination.
