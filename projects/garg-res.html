Garg Residence, Faridabad

Project typeResidential
LocationFaridabad, Haryana
Involvement StageProject Inception to Schematic Design
RoleInterior Architect
During my tenure atSixteen Design Studio

This residential block is designed for two brothers’ families, with planning rooted in client-specific requirements and Vaastu principles. Biophilic elements and a restrained luxury palette enhance both interiors and exteriors, resulting in a harmonious blend of functionality, privacy, and refined living.

Organized around a central courtyard, the layout is clearly zoned to balance shared and private living. The entrance and front areas function as common spaces for both families, while the rear portion is symmetrically divided by a centrally placed staircase and lift, creating two independent residential zones. Each family unit is fully self-sufficient, with its own kitchen and living spaces.
