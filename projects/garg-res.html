Garg Residence, Faridabad

Project typeResidential
LocationFaridabad, Haryana
Involvement StageDesign Development to Construction Phase
RoleInterior Architect
During my tenure atSixteen Design Studio

For minimalism, shade is more important than color. The pale e is neutral, common for natural materials. Shades of white, beige, brown, gray are used. Simple geometric shapes and clear volumes are preferred. Special attention is paid to the light-related effects. Therefore, the wall-sized windows and flat roofs with glass fragments are designed the hidden light along the perimeter of the space is widely used.

Inside, the villa blends rustic warmth with understated modern comfort. Sunlit rooms, breezy verandas, and a private pool create an easy, slow living atmosphere ideal for families and small groups. Balanced materials and clear spatial flow make it equally inviting as a home and an Airbnb escape.

A serene Goan hideaway shaped by landscape, light, and quiet Mediterranean charm.
