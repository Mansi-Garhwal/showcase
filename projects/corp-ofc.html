Keshwani Corporate House, Ahmedabad

Project typeCommercial
LocationAhmedabad, Gujarat
Involvement StageProject Inception & Concept Design
RoleArchitect
During my tenure atWAL+L

Conceived around the idea of a diagonal split, the design transforms a simple form into a bold architectural gesture. The structure integrates a walkable green roof that blends seamlessly with the surrounding landscape, creating continuity between built and natural environments. Large openings invite daylight and greenery indoors, enhancing spatial fluidity and interaction. The result is a workspace that balances geometry with spontaneity where circulation, light, and landscape converge to foster creativity and connection.

An expressive workplace where form meets play, and architecture grows with the landscape.
