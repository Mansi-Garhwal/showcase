Ghumghawa Farmhouse, Raipur

Project typeResidential
LocationRaipur, Chhattisgarh
Involvement StageProject Inception to Schematic Design
RoleInterior Architect
During my tenure atSixteen Design Studio

Set along the riverbank, this farmhouse draws inspiration from the form and movement of a boat, translating the idea into a linear, flowing architectural composition. The entrance is marked by a waterbody with a boat installation, establishing a strong thematic arrival and a visual connection to the river beyond. The building mass stretches parallel to the landscape, with open decks and glazed facades framing uninterrupted views of water and greenery.

The outdoor experience is anchored by an infinity pool and deck positioned at the riverâ€™s edge, designed to visually merge with the flowing river beyond. This seamless transition between built form, water, and landscape enhances the sense of calm and continuity, while the material palette and horizontal lines reinforce the nautical narrative. The farmhouse becomes a retreat where architecture, nature, and water exist in quiet dialogue.
