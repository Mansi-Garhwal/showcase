Tipsy Bull, Koramangala, Bengaluru

Project typeHospitality
LocationKoramangala, Bengaluru, Karnataka
Involvement StageDesign Development to Handover
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atACME Designers

Inspired by the energy of a stock exchange, this multilevel bar unfolds through interconnected floors, playful cutouts, and unobstructed views of a central DJ booth that anchors the entire experience. Bold colors, barcode styled bar counters, and a dramatic currency note chandelier set the tone for a lively, thematic environment. Interactive elements such as crossword integrations and dynamic graphics amplify the fun, creating a space that feels energetic, social, and constantly in motion. The exterior introduces this vibrancy with a striking black façade, neon accents, spinning blocks wrapped in currency prints, and a stylized stock market arrow marking the entry.

My contribution focused on creating Detail Shop Drawings of furnitures, DJ & Bar counters, and design elements, such as, cut-out metal railings, façade spinning blocks railings and staircase. Additionally, I conducted site visits during construction and installation for supervision and coordination.
