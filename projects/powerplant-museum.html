Power Plant Museum, Patna

Project typeCommercial
LocationPatna, Bihar
Involvement StageProject Inception & Concept Design
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atACME Designers

This museum presents an immersive journey through power generation, showcasing how energy is produced, how it has evolved, and how sustainable technologies are shaping the future. Across 30 themed zones, visitors move from mythological ideas of lightning to atoms, magnetism, current systems, and modern innovations like smart cities and clean energy. Each exhibit uses interactive models to explain powerplant functioning, energy extraction from various sources, and the growing importance of sustainability and innovation.

The spatial design blends industrial textures, greenery, and water bodies, echoing the feel of a real power facility. Functional indoor & outdoor setups of solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and thermal systems allow visitors to experience power generation firsthand. Smart homes, EV displays, and human-powered stations offer deeper engagement, reinforcing the museum’s mission to educate and inspire awareness about energy and its future.
