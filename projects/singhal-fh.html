Singhal Farmhouse, Raipur

Project typeResidential
LocationRaipur, Chhattisgarh
Involvement StageProject Inception to Schematic Design
RoleDesign Architect
During my tenure atSixteen Design Studio

This farmhouse comprises two identical residential blocks designed for two brother's families, accommodating three generations with equal emphasis on privacy and shared living. Planned with client-specific requirements and Vaastu alignment, each block features a grand entrance, double-height living spaces, central courtyard and generous outdoor areas that enhance openness and connection to nature.

An existing structure on the site is retained and repurposed as a shared recreational building, strengthening interaction between both families. Together, the old and new structures create a balanced environment that supports family life, leisure, and refined farmhouse living.
