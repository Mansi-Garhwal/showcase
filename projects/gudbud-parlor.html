Gudbud Icecream Parlor, Bengaluru

Project typeHospitality
LocationKoramangala, Bengaluru, Karnataka
Involvement StagePre-Design to Handover
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atACME Designers

Designed as an Ice Cream Town Square, this boutique parlor captures the essence of joy and nostalgia through a playful yet refined design language. The space is immersed in cheerful pastel tones as baby pink, powder blue, and mint creating a bright and inviting ambiance. Mirrors framed with golden strip detailing expand the perceived volume, while illustrated wall murals and candy-colored décor evoke a whimsical, storybook charm.

Wooden shelving with gold-finish accents display miniatures and candy jars, adding depth and warmth to the vibrant setting. Cloud installations suspended from the ceiling enhance the dreamy atmosphere, blending fantasy with fun. The pastel toned metal seating, small café tables, and cozy layout encourage comfort and casual interaction making the space both kid-friendly and Insta-worthy.
