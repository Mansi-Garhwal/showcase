Shukla Residence, Kanpur

Project typeResidential
LocationKanpur, Uttar Pradesh
Involvement StageUp to Schematic Development
RoleArchitect
During my tenure atWAL+L

The plot, jointly owned by two brothers, was planned with foresight to allow future subdivision while functioning cohesively in the present. The design accommodates two independent residences within a unified framework, each equipped with expansive lawns, double-height spaces, and amenities tailored for comfortable, lavish living.

Architecturally, the residence expresses a refined interplay of glass, texture, and proportion. Expansive openings balance transparency and privacy, while crisp geometry and subtle material transitions lend rhythm to the elevation. Thoughtfully articulated floor levels and landscaped edges create movement and depth, and inside, symmetry and scale guide a tactile, contemporary spatial experience.
