Verlekar Residence, Margao

Project typeResidential
LocationMargao, Goa
Involvement StageDesign Development & Construction Phase
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atMSS Design

Conceived as a “concrete luxury home”, this Residence derives its name and character from the material that defines it 'concrete'. The design translates European contemporary sensibilities into a bold yet refined architectural expression, where structure, texture, and technology coexist seamlessly. The residence integrates full home automation, enhancing comfort and embodying a sophisticated, modern lifestyle.

Spatially, the plan is organized into three distinct zones, live-in, relax, and reside, ensuring functional clarity and fluid movement. A subtle site slope is utilized efficiently, creating effortless access between the ground and basement levels. Interiors reflect a minimal yet elegant finish, pairing the raw honesty of concrete with delicate details and soft tonal balance. The result is a home that embodies strength, precision, and understated luxury.

A symphony of concrete and comfort, where contemporary design meets effortless sophistication.
