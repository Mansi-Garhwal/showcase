Government Boys Hostel, Khandola

Project typeInstitutional
LocationKhandola, Goa
Involvement StageGFC & Tendering Drawings
RoleArchitecture Intern
During my tenure atMSS Design

Designed with simplicity and functionality at its core, the Boys Government Hostel redefines the conventional idea of student living by placing community and interaction at the heart of its layout. The plan revolves around a central courtyard that seamlessly connects all rooms, transforming circulation into a social experience rather than a mere passage. This open, interconnected design encourages engagement and inclusivity while maintaining privacy and ease of movement. Prioritizing the human scale, every element, from room orientation to common zones, is thoughtfully proportioned to enhance comfort, accessibility, and belonging. The architecture’s restrained vocabulary ensures that form follows function, creating an environment that supports both individuality and collective living.

A simple yet thoughtful design, nurturing community through shared space and human connection.
